<ul><li>Sort the data by gender:</li><li>Type Control-A to select all of the data in the worksheet.</li><li>Choose <code style="inline">Data -&gt; Sort</code> from the menubar.</li><li>In the pop-up window, under <code style="inline">Sort by</code> choose <code style="inline">gender</code>, make sure <code style="inline">Header row</code> is selected towards the bottom of the window, and  click <code style="inline">OK</code>.</li><li>Now select all of the data in columns B and C, and then choose <code style="inline">Insert -&gt; Chart</code> from the top menubar.</li><li>Choose <code style="inline">XY (Scatter)</code> and then Click <code style="inline">Finish</code>.</li><li>Now we have a scatterplot, but the data is all on the right of the plot.  To fix this:</li><li>Right-click on the x-axis, and choose <code style="inline">Format Axis</code> from the pop-up menu.</li><li>Click the tab labeled <code style="inline">Scale</code>, type <code style="inline">50</code> in the box next to <code style="inline">Minimum</code>, and click <code style="inline">OK</code>.</li></ul>
