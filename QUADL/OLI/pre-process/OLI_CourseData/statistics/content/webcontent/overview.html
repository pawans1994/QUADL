Course Overview         

This course is an online version of 36-201 (Introductory Statistics), and as such provides the course material, learning activities, opportunity for self assessment (checking whether you are "getting" the material), and real assessments (where we get to see how well you are getting the material.) Below you'll find explanations about important technical features of the course, which will help you navigate through it and take advantage of all the type of activities it offers.

Navigation:
You can use the right and left arrows in the navigation bar at the top or bottom of the screen to move from page to page in the course.

You can also use the navigation frame on the left to quickly move through course topics.

Images/Graphs:
In many places throughout the course, you will be asked to answer questions about an image or graph that has been presented to you. To keep from having to scroll up and down the page (from question to image and back), just click on the image and it will open in a separate window. This way, once you arrange the windows, you should be able to see the image at the same time that you answer the question.

Learning Opportunities/Activities:
Sprinkled throughout the course are buttons that represent additional learning opportunities or activities:

  • Module Objectives - On most pages throughout the course, you can see the learning objectives for the current module by clicking on the objectives button in the top or bottom nav bar.

  • Learn By Doing - Periodically, you will be given opportunities to practice what you have learned. These activities typically contain instructions for performing an activity in Minitab, and then ask you questions about the activity. You can then compare your answers with answers that the instructor would have given.

    Learn By Doing  

  • Did I Get This? - Throughout the course, there are opportunities for you to take self-assessments to gauge your understanding of the content. These self-assessments do not count towards your grade, and can be taken as many times as desired.

    Did I Get This?  

  • Many Students Wonder... - This button will launch a page that contains frequently asked questions. These questions generally address common misconceptions that students have, or clarify points that have been made in the main page.

    Many Students Wonder...  

  • StatTutor - StatTutor is a web-based intelligent tutor that provides you with a data analysis problem, supports you as you attempt to solve it, and gives hints and feedback along the way.

    StatTutor  

  • Quiz - At the end of each module, you will find a Quiz. The quizzes are similar to the "Did I Get This" activities, but count for a grade. They may only be taken one time.

    Quiz
