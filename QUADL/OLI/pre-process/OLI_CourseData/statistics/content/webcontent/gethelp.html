Get Help      

Having problems with some aspect of the course? Here are some ways to get help:

I Need Help With the Course Content
  • For an explanation of how the content of this course is structured (learning activities, quizzes, etc.) please see the Course Overview.
  • If you have other questions related to the content of the course, please email your instructor, Bryce Corrigan, at bryce@cmu.edu
I'm Having Technical Problems
  • For help configuring your system to work with this course, please see Test & Configure Your System.
  • If, after reading the "Test & Configure Your System" page, you are still having technical problems, you should email oli-help@lists.andrew.cmu.edu for assistance. Please include a detailed description of the problem, and tell us what operating system (example: Windows XP or Mac OS) and browser version you're using (example: Internet Explorer 6.0)
