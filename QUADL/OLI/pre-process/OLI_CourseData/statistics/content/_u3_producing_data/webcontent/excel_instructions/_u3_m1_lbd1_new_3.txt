<ul><li>Click on one of the cells in your table and then click the <code style="inline">PivotChart</code> button in the <code style="inline">Tools</code> group on the <code style="inline">Options (PivotTable Tools)</code> tab.</li><li> In the <code style="inline">Insert Chart</code> window that appears, choose the first pie chart option and click <code style="inline">OK</code>.</li><li>To label the sections of the pie, right click the pie and choose <code style="inline">Add Data Labels</code> from the pop-up menu.</li><li>To see percentages as well as absolute values, right-click one of the data labels and choose <code style="inline">Format Data Labels</code>.  Then make sure that <code style="inline">Label Options</code> is selected on the left and check the box next to <code style="inline">Percentage</code> and click <code style="inline">Close</code>.</li></ul>
