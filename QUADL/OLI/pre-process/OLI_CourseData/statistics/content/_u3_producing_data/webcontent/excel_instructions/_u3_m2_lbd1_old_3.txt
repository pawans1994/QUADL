<ul><li>To create this table, first make the row and column labels: in an empty section of your worksheet, pick a cell and type <code style="inline">Statistic</code>.  Then move one cell to the right and type <code style="inline">Treatment 1</code>.  Move one cell to the right and type <code style="inline">Treatment 2</code>, and then another cell to the right and type <code style="inline">Treatment 3</code>.  Now, in the cell  underneath <code style="inline">Statistic</code>, type <code style="inline">Q1</code>.  Move down one cell and type <code style="inline">min</code>, then underneath that <code style="inline">Median</code>, then <code style="inline">Max</code>, and finally <code style="inline">Q3</code>.  </li><li>Note that the order here is slightly different than the ordering we've been using - the reason is that the statistical measures have to be  in this order (Q1, min, Median, Max, Q3) for Excel to graph the boxplot correctly.</li></ul>
