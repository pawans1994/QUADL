<ul><li>Pick an empty column in your Excel worksheet and label it <code style="inline">Treatment</code>.</li><li>In the <code style="inline">Treatment</code> column, type this formula in row 2: <code style="inline">=RANDBETWEEN(1,3)</code>. This generates a random integer from 1 to 3 in each cell of the column.</li><li>Select the entire column of random numbers that you just created, right-click on the column and choose <code style="inline">Copy</code>, and then right-click on the column again and choose <code style="inline">Paste Special</code> .</li><li>In the pop-up window, choose <code style="inline">Values</code> under <code style="inline">Paste</code>, and click <code style="inline">OK</code>.</li><li>Type Control-A twice to select all of the data in the worksheet, and then choose <code style="inline">Sort &amp; Filter -&gt; Custom Sort</code> from the <code style="inline">Editing</code> group in the <code style="inline">Home</code> tab.</li><li>In the pop-up window, make sure that <code style="inline">My data has headers</code> is checked and then choose <code style="inline">Treatment</code> from the pull-down menu next to <code style="inline">Sort by</code>.
