<ul><li>Highlight the table you just created, including the row and column labels <code style="inline">but not the rows containing St Dev and n</code>, and then select <code style="inline">Insert -&gt; Chart</code> from the top menubar. </li><li>When the Chart Wizard window opens, select <code style="inline">Line Chart</code>, and then click <code style="inline">Next</code>.  In Step 2, make sure <code style="inline">Rows</code> is selected, and then click <code style="inline">Finish</code>.</li><li>What we have now is a line chart, because Excel can't generate boxplots directly, but we're going to make boxplots from this line chart. </li><li>Right click on any line on the graph and choose <code style="inline">Format Data Series</code> from the pop-up menu.
