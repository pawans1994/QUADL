<ul><li>To create a scatterplot:</li><li>Select all of the data in columns A and B, and then choose <code style="inline">Insert -&gt; Chart</code> from the top menubar.</li><li>Choose <code style="inline">XY (Scatter)</code> and then Click <code style="inline">Finish</code>. </li><li>Remember that if you like, you can adjust the axes to better see the	data by double-clicking on an axis, choosing the tab marked <code style="inline">Scale</code>, and setting the minimum and maximum to something more appropriate than what Excel chose for you.</li><li>To compute the correlation:</li><li>Click in a cell outside of the first two columns of data.</li><li>Type <code style="inline">=correl([range1],[range2])</code> where [range1] is the first column of data and [range2] is the second column of data.</li></ul>
