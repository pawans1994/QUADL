<ul><li>Right-click on one of the data points in the scatterplot, and choose <code style="inline">Add Trendline</code> from the pop-up menu.</li><li>Make sure that <code style="inline">Trendline Options</code> is selected on the left, and then choose <code style="inline">Linear</code> under <code style="inline">Trend/Regression type</code>.</li><li>At the bottom of the window, make sure that <code style="inline">Display Equation on chart</code> and <code style="inline">Display R-squared value on chart</code> are checked.</li><li>Click <code style="inline">Close</code>.</li></ul>
