<ul><li>Open Excel with a new worksheet.</li><li>Choose <code style="inline">Tools -&gt; Data Analysis</code> from the menubar.  In the pop-up window, choose <code style="inline">Random Number Generation</code>.</li><li>In the next window, enter 250 next to <code style="inline">Number of Variables</code>.  Enter 100 next to <code style="inline">Number of Random Numbers</code>.  Choose <code style="inline">Bernoulli</code> from the <code style="inline">Distribution</code> pull-down menu.  Enter 0.6 next to <code style="inline">p Value</code>.  Finally, make sure <code style="inline">Output Range</code> is selected, and enter A1 next to it.  Click <code style="inline">OK</code>.</li><li>Look at the worksheet.  Frightening, isn't it!</li></ul>
