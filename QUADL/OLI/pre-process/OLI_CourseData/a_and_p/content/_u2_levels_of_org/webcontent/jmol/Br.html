These features were noted in Lecture:
 1. The protein has seven a-helices that span the bilayer.
 2. The membrane-exposed surface is highly hydrophobic.
 3. The charged/polar residues are at the solvent interfaces and also line the internal hydrophilic channel.  Polar residues utilized for H+ transport (Ball & Stick).
