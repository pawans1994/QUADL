Water

  • The two lone pair orbitals coloured yellow have a partial negative charge (click on 'Single Molecule').
  • The two hydrogens carry a partial positive charge.
  • Hydrogen bonds are formed between the electronegative oxygen and electropositive hydrogen.
  • Note that many of the hydrogen bonds observed in ice are maintained in liquid water.
