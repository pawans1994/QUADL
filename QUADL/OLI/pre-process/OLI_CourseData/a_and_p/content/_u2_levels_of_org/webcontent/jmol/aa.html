Determining Chirality

  1. Label the four groups attached to the chiral center with the letters W,X,Y,Z. W has the highest atomic number (NH group).
  2. Point the group labeled 'Z' away from you (Ha here).
  3. If W,X,Y is counter-clockwise the compound is L, or S.
  4. If W,X,Y is clockwise the compound is D, or R. Most common amino acids are L.
