Viewing Suggestions

  1. The button labeled "Trp (yellow)/Chymo (green) will show trypsin superimposed on chymotrypsin. How are they the same? How do they differ?
  2. 'Trypsin': Displays Trypsin only.
  3. Add the inhibitor (chymotrypsin) or substrate (trypsin) and view the difference in substrate specificity. The bound inhibitor or substrate is rendered in "ball and stick" and is dark gray in color. What residues in chymotrypsin contact the sidechain of the substrate? Which in trypsin?
