VIEWING SUGGESTIONS
  • Catalysis: Note the catalytic triad (Asp, His, Ser). Add the substrate to see how close the Ser is to the peptide bond.
  • Enzyme - Substrate Interactions: Select the solid button 'Specificity' to generate a surface that highlights the specificity pocket. +/- surface toggles the display of the surface. +/- translucent changes the opacity of the surface. Note the interaction of + charge on substrate with negative charge (Asp 189) on the enzyme.
  • Select 'Substrate surface' to generate a surface over the substrate.
  • Select 'Enz. surface' to generate a surface over the entire enzyme.
  • Remove the substrate surface to see the deep specificity pocket.
