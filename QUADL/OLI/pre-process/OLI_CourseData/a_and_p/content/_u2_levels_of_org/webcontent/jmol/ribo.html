Key Points: 30s 50s tRNA sites
 
  • 16S rRNA
  • Binds mRNA and recognizes SD sequence
  • 23S and 5S rRNA
  • Responsible for formation of peptide bond (Ribozyme).
  • A site: Contains amino-acyl tRNA that is to be added to chain.
  • P site: Contains growing peptide attached to tRNA.
  • E site: Discharged tRNA exits from here.
